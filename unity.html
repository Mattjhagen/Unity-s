Adulting has become too damndifficult!

You work so hard to keepeverythingtogether.Relationships, school, career, bills, childrenyou name it, you try to manage itall!And maybe to most, youveheld it togetherfor a long time.

What you dont allow others to see is howagonizing it feels to keep up thecharade. You hide thetearsand put on a smile as you prepare for the nextBS experience.Its just another day where you put yourselflast!

To avoid theemotional painyou find yourself focusing on others.You seekworthinessin accomplishments, you people please while ignoringYOURneeds, and youve become aperfectionist.Youre tired of giving and giving with nothing in return. All the while, you tell yourself that yourenotgood enough. You run through your list of failures like a highlight reel!

Failureisnt an option and,lets face it, you cant afford anotherdisappointment.Life has taught you toavoidfeelings and keep it movingbut now you feel like you cant. As theanxietycreeps in like a dense fog, you feel the 1,000 pound weight on your chest get heavier, it feels like you are sinking in quick-sand, and youover-thinkeverything.

Now at a crossroad, you have a choice of succumbing to the cripplingemotionsyou have run from yourentirelife or climb out of that quick-sand.Therapy can help you organize your thoughts, heal thehurt, and stop the madness. You can finally put all of this unbearable mess behind you.

You just want to feel better!

We understand how the stress and the high expectations of being a grown-up can be too much. We are passionate about working with adults struggling to fit all the pieces together in a unified way.

Lets put an end to the endless battles and put you in a place where you feel confident again.Together, we can make the overwhelming conflicts a thing of the past. If youre ready to feel better, ditchthe laundry list of crap you repeat to yourself, and climb out of that sinking pit We can help!
