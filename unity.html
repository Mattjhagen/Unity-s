CLICK HERE FOR ADULT SERVICES

Adulting has become too damn difficult!

You work so hard to keep everything together. Relationships, school, career, bills, children…you name it, you try to manage it all! And maybe to most, you’ve held it together for a long time.

What you don’t allow others to see is how agonizing it feels to keep up the charade.  You hide the tears and put on a smile as you prepare for the next BS experience. It’s just another day where you put yourself last!

To avoid the emotional pain you find yourself focusing on others. You seek worthiness in accomplishments, you people please while ignoring YOUR needs, and you’ve become a perfectionist. You’re tired of giving and giving with nothing in return. All the while, you tell yourself that you’re not good enough. You run through your list of failures like a highlight reel!

Failure isn’t an option and, let’s face it, you can’t afford another disappointment. Life has taught you to avoid feelings and keep it moving…but now you feel like you can’t. As the anxiety creeps in like a dense fog, you feel the 1,000 pound weight on your chest get heavier, it feels like you are sinking in quick-sand, and you over-think everything. 

Now at a crossroad, you have a choice of succumbing to the crippling emotions you have run from your entire life or climb out of that quick-sand. Therapy can help you organize your thoughts, heal the hurt, and stop the madness. You can finally put all of this unbearable mess behind you.

You just want to feel better!

We understand how the stress and the high expectations of being a grown-up can be too much. We are passionate about working with adults struggling to fit all the pieces together in a unified way. 

Let’s put an end to the endless battles and put you in a place where you feel confident again. Together, we can make the overwhelming conflicts a thing of the past. If you’re ready to feel better, ditch the laundry list of crap you repeat to yourself, and climb out of that sinking pit – We can help!
