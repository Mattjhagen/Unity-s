Meet Laura Long

I Help Stressed Out, High Performers Find Balance And Fulfillment.

Others view you as a hard worker, someone whos dedicated, reliable, and gets stuff done.

You have high standards for others, and even higher standards for yourself.

Youve accomplished almost everything youve ever put your mind to, despite the many obstacles that stood in your way.

As far as anyone on the outside knows, you have a great life. Money doesnt appear to be an issue, and it looks like you have a supportive family that you get to come home to every night

But you feel empty. Unfulfilled. Exhausted.

This whole “work-life balance” thing doesnt work when you wear both hats 24/7, so it always feels like youre letting someone down.

Youre ready to get off the rollercoaster so that you can enjoy the life youve worked so hard to create for yourself and your family. If youre feeling burned out, anxious, or paralyzed by perfectionism, youve come to the right place.

Your Time Is Extremely Valuable

Therapy should never feel like just another weekly obligation, so Ive made it easier than ever to get help.

With24/7 online scheduling, an onlineclient portal, and the ability to doonline therapythrough a secure platform for those who often travel for work (or who have trouble getting out of the office), therapy has never been this easy.

Please take a look around and if you like what you see, give me a call or schedule afree, no obligation 20-minute phone consultation. Id love to help you get started today.

Here Are My Specialties:

Stress & Anxiety

Overwhelm, perfectionism, and insomnia have swooped in and taken over, leaving you in an almost constant state of stress. Therapy will help you identify the underlying patterns that keep you stuck and give you actionable tools to fight back.

Entrepreneurial Burnout

You didnt realize you were walking a tightrope until you fell off. Youve made so many sacrifices to get your business to where it is today, but those sacrifices came at a high cost. You want to fall back in love with your work while also figuring out how to prevent burnout in the future.

Clinical Supervision

So you passed the MFT exam congratulations! Youre in the final stretch towards licensure, and Im here to help you get there. As an SC-Approved LMFT Supervisor, I can provide all of your supervision requirements (and well have fun, too).

What Makes Me Different?

As a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, Ive received specialized training in treating every person as a whole, which includes all of the important relationships in their life. I focus on helping you create healthy, meaningful connections with those closest to you.

Quality

I maintain a small caseload so that I can provide the highest quality of care to my clients. Im also easily accessible in between sessions.

Efficiency

The last thing you need is a passive, head-nodding therapist. My goal is to help you begin to see progress as soon as possible.

Convenience

My secure client portal allows for online scheduling, instant messaging, and keeping track of your billing statements all with the click of a button.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where Are You Located?

My practice is located at Five Forks Counseling Group, 205 Bryce Circle, Suite B. My practice can be found in the Woodruff Place business park, right in the heart of Five Forks (Simpsonville).

Do You Have Evening Appointments Available?

While I do my best work in the mornings and early afternoons, I do have some late afternoon slots available. These typically book up weeks in advance, so if you have any flexibility in your schedule, I can fit you in much sooner. Click Here to learn more about my availability.

Do You See Couples?

Yes! My practice serves couples as well as individuals. With couples, I'm able to help with communication issues, blended family dynamics, and pre-marital counseling. I'm also trained in discernment counseling, which is a structured, 1-5 session protocol to help couples in crisis.

How Long Does Therapy Last?

As short as possible and as long as it takes. I consider myself a brief therapist, so I want to give you what you need and get you on your way. Most of my clients are able to see progress within a few short months.

Do You Take Insurance?

I do not participate as an in-network provider, but you have options. See the Investment page for more details about how you can make therapy work for you and your budget.

Are You A Christian Counselor?

My training is in evidence-based practices, and I do not practice as a Christian Counselor. I am open to discussing religion and spirituality if its important to you, but if you dont consider yourself spiritual or religious, this is also a safe space for you.
